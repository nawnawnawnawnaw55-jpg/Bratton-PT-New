How Physical Therapy Helps Arthritis

  • Strengthening the muscles that support your joints reduces joint stress
  • Manual therapy improves joint mobility and reduces stiffness
  • Low-impact exercise maintains cartilage health
  • Education on activity modification and joint protection strategies
  • Balance and gait training to reduce fall risk
  • Pain management without relying solely on medication
