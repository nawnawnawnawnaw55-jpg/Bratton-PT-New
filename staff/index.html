Our Team

At Bratton Physical Therapy, every patient works directly with a licensed physical therapist — never passed off to an aide or tech. Our team brings years of experience and a commitment to personalized, one-on-one care.

PT

Licensed Physical Therapists

Doctorate-level training with specialized certifications in manual therapy, dry needling, and sports rehabilitation.

1:1

One-on-One Model

Your therapist is with you for the entire session — no double-booking, no handing off to assistants.

Our team stays current with the latest evidence-based techniques through ongoing training and certification.
